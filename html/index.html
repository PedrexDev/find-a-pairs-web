Useful commands!

Aumb has a lot of commands that will show you some pretty useful information. And Aumb has a lot of them! Do you want to know the current COVID statistics from your state? Not a problem! Just type in /covid [state] and you'll have your state's COVID stats in a few moments! And there is much more, just look in the help menu!

Fun commands!

Aumb is literally crammed with commands that are semi-useful. You can, for example, use the command /animal [animalName] to look at the picture of your chosen animal. Or send a funny meme via /meme [memeCategory]! Or you can type the command /snake and a new instance of the Snake game will be launched on your server for you to play! The possibilities are almost limitless.

Simple moderation!

Aumb comes with commands that make moderation on your server a breeze! Just specify if you want to ban someone, kick mute, and so on!
